Membership in the project is open to any participant willing to commit to the roles and responsibilities identified by the working group to support the overall success of the initiative. Contributions may include, but are not limited to:

  • Knowledge of the subject matter
  • Scalability of implementing the eventual solution
  • Willingness to assist in the delivery of key outputs and working group artifacts
  • Developing a testing framework for reference implementations
  • Testing

Next steps?

  • Join our weekly calls - contact the administrator for an invite
  • Submit general questions to the PACIO Project email list
  • Review the project charter, meeting minutes and other materials on GitHub
  • Join our slack channel and its ongoing conversation threads
  • Help spread the word about the PACIO Project by retweeting major announcements
  • Submit a pull request via GitHub
